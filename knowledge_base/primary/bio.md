## Long Bio (500 words)
Ioanna Renta is a VP of Engineering and technology executive whose career has been defined by one consistent pattern: walking into complex, high-stakes engineering situations and making them measurably better.

Her career began in the world of safety-critical systems at Siemens/Unify, where she wrote C and assembly for telecoms platforms, led M2M Operations, and served as Crisis Manager and NOC leader. This foundation in systems thinking — where failure has real consequences — shaped everything that followed.

At HERE Technologies, Ioanna led the AUTO SDK team through one of the most consequential deliveries of her career: the AUDI A8 navigation programme. The customer relationship was at risk, the software was behind schedule, and the production line date was fixed. She kept the customer, shipped the software on time, and as part of the engineering work her team reduced navigation cold-start time from 55 seconds to 5 seconds through tile caching and rendering pipeline optimisation.

At Delivery Hero, she took on the Restaurants domain — a 240+ engineer organisation spanning multiple countries — and immediately focused on what she has always focused on first: making invisible problems visible. She designed and implemented the Quality Maturity Index (QMI), an eight-dimension engineering health framework. Within six months, 50% of the broader organisation had voluntarily adopted it — proof that measurement, done right, is compelling rather than coercive. During her tenure she also drove 35% organic growth and a 60% retention increase through ML-powered recommendations, served as Global Incident Commander during major incidents, and established a 60/40 tech debt allocation model.

At sennder, she led Platform & Data engineering for 150+ engineers through a period of significant transformation. She authored the Engineering Manifesto that defined how the organisation would work, migrated a distributed monolith to modern architecture in six months, reduced P1 incidents from 20/week to 2/month, and led the organisation's AI tooling adoption including LibreChat and MCP server rollout for commercial email parsing and knowledge management.

Throughout her career, Ioanna has maintained zero regrettable attrition across every organisation she has led — a metric she considers the most honest signal of engineering culture health. She is known for pre-emptive scaling, iterative pragmatic modernisation over big-bang rewrites, and a leadership style she describes as "see the problem clearly, solve it structurally, build the patterns."

She holds an MBA, an MSc in Telecommunications, a BSc in Physics, a Generative AI Leader certification from Google (2026), and a No-Code AI & Machine Learning certification from MIT (2023). She is based in Berlin and actively pursuing VP/CTO roles in the European technology sector.
